    What is COVID-19?

    COVID-19 is a contagious illness that is caused by a virus. Since no humans are immune to this disease, COVID-19 is very dangerous. Along with that, there is no vaccine that can be used to reduce it.

    Symptoms

    Those who have been infected with Coronavirus have most likely shown symptoms:

    • Cough
    • Fever
    • Shortness of Breath

    Other symptoms include:

    • Muscle ache
    • Chills
    • Fatigue
    • Headache
    • Stomach issues (ex. diarrhea)
