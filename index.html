    OUR MISSION

    Using coding , COVID WATCH's team aims to help decrease coronavirus cases across the country and across the world. Using our local COVID-19 map tracker which we plan to expand across the world in the future, you will be able to track the amount of people in your area. This tool will help you make easy and reasonable decisions before entering a supermarket, appliance store, and even somebody's house by detecting hotspots before you make the trip.
